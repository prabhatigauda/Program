About Guitar

The guitar is a fretted musical instrument that typically has six strings. It is held flat against the player's body and played by strumming or plucking the strings with the dominant hand, while simultaneously pressing selected strings against frets with the fingers of the opposite hand.

Popularity Guitar Facts

  • Guitar music first exploded in the late 1800s.
  • It's the world's second most popular instrument.
  • The word “guitar” has Sanskrit roots.
  • The oldest guitar-like instruments came from Egypt and Mesopotamia.
  • The oldest image of a guitar-like instrument was found in Turkey.
